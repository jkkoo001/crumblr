About Us

crumblr is the leading bakery in Singapore

Established in 2022, crumblr came from very humble beginnings. Started off as a home-based business, crumblr quickly gained traction by word-of-mouth , soon after, set up it's own brick-and-mortar. Offering a selection of bakes and pastries, we provide customers in the city a whiff of our baked goods throughout the day.

Our Purpose

We started with a sole purpose of creating breads for everyone with the highest quality natural ingredients and at accessible locations with affordable prices.

We produce breads, cakes and pastries 360 days of the year. Our bakers begin the day early with the freshest doughs to be handmade and baked fresh daily onsite so that our customers across the city can catch a whiff of our baked goods throughout the day.
